What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. It encompasses a variety of technologies and approaches, including machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

Applications of AI

AI is used in various fields, including healthcare, finance, education, and more. Some common applications include:

  • Speech recognition
  • Image and video analysis
  • Autonomous vehicles
  • Chatbots and virtual assistants

Future of AI

The field of AI is rapidly evolving, and researchers are exploring new possibilities every day. As AI continues to advance, it is expected to play a crucial role in shaping the future of technology and society.
